Much-loved village carol singing with the community, attended by the Mayor of Basingstoke and Deane. Taking place at Village Pond, East Oakley.

Donations support local groups and organisations.

Join us for this cherished annual tradition as we gather around the village pond for festive carol singing. A wonderful community event that brings everyone together to celebrate the Christmas season.
